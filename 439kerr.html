Location: 439 Kerr St, Oakville, Ontario

Wonderful One Bedroom apartment for rent located in downtown Oakville, on Kerr St. Apartment features a large skylight which allows a lot of natural light in throughout the year. Large windows in the living room and bedroom as well. Original hardwood flooring in the living room and bedroom. Short walk to Oakville GO Station. Apartment features newer appliances with updated kitchen countertops and backsplash. Parking for one car in private parking lot is also available.
